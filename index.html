Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Birthdate Calculator app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Information We Collect

We collect non-personal information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage patterns and interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising identifiers (for ad personalization)

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase automatically collects and processes information including:

  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information
  • Geographic location (if permitted)
  • Performance metrics

2.2 Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports and performance data to help us improve app stability and fix issues. This includes:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device state information
  • Performance metrics

2.3 AdMob Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Geographic location
You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Our app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Calculation history
  • Uploaded photos (if any)
This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Control app permissions

6. Notifications

Our app may send notifications for:

  • Birthday reminders
  • App updates
  • Feature announcements
You can manage notifications through your device settings.

7. In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases processed through:

  • Google Play Store (Android)
  • App Store (iOS)
Payment information is handled by these platforms, not our app.

8. Data Retention

We retain non-personal data collected through Firebase and AdMob as long as necessary for:

  • App improvement
  • Performance optimization
  • Ad delivery

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the app with notification
  • Sending an email (if provided)

10. Compliance

Our app complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • App Store and Google Play Store policies

11. Contact Us

Email: calculatebirthdate@gmail.com

12. Data Deletion

You can delete your app data in these ways:
