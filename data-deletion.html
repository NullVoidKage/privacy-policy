Data Deletion Instructions

This page provides instructions on how to delete your data from the Birthdate Calculator app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and making data deletion as simple as possible.

1. In-App Deletion

Simple Steps to Delete Your Data

  • Open Birthdate Calculator app
  • Go to Settings menu
  • Select "Delete My Data"
  • Confirm deletion when prompted
  • Restart the app to verify deletion

2. Device Settings Method

For Android Users

  • Open your device Settings
  • Go to Apps or Application Manager
  • Find "Birthdate Calculator"
  • Tap Storage
  • Select "Clear Data"

For iOS Users

  • Open your device Settings
  • Find "Birthdate Calculator"
  • Select "Reset App"
  • Confirm the reset

3. Email Request

You can also request data deletion by:

  • Sending an email to: calculatebirthdate@gmail.com
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include your device type and app version
We will process your request within 48 hours.

4. Data Types Deleted

The following data will be deleted:

  • Saved calculations and history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Local cache and temporary files
  • Any stored notifications or reminders

5. Verification

To verify successful data deletion:

  • Close the app completely
  • Remove it from recent apps
  • Reopen the app
  • All settings should be reset to default

6. Important Notes

Please be aware that:

  • Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is retained on our servers
  • App Store/Play Store reviews are not affected

7. Need Help?

If you need assistance with data deletion:

  • Email us at: calculatebirthdate@gmail.com
  • Include "Help with Data Deletion" in subject
  • We'll respond within 48 hours
